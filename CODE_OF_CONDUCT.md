## Contributor Covenant

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to respect all people who contribute through reporting issues, posting feature requests, updating documentation, submitting pull requests or patches, and other activities.

Be kind, welcome feedback, and assume good intent. Harassment and abusive behavior will not be tolerated. For full text and reporting instructions, see https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers at the project issue tracker or open a private issue addressed to the maintainers.
